About Us

Your trusted healthcare transportation partner in Antalya, Turkey. Specialized in medical tourism transfers with a commitment to patient comfort and safety.

Our Story

A Decade of Excellence in Healthcare Transportation

AYTU OTOMOTİV A.Ş was founded with a clear vision: to become Antalya's leading transportation provider for the rapidly growing medical tourism industry.

As Antalya emerged as a world-class destination for dental care, aesthetic surgery, eye treatments, and wellness tourism, we recognized the need for a specialized, patient-focused transportation service. We built our company to bridge the gap between international airports and medical facilities with comfort, safety, and professionalism.

Today, with over 150 vehicles across 7 locations and partnerships with 50+ clinics and hospitals, we are the trusted choice for healthcare travelers from Europe, the Middle East, and beyond. Our multilingual team and specialized fleet ensure every patient's journey is smooth from arrival to departure.
